# agents

## Concepts
- [content-signal](content-signal.md) -- Scores the page's Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trust signals; judges citation-worthiness and answer front-loading for AI search.
- [crawlability](crawlability.md) -- Judges how much content is blocked or invisible to AI crawlers — robots.txt restrictions, JS-gated content, sitemap gaps — and produces plain-language fixes.
- [entity-topic](entity-topic.md) -- Maps the page's topic-to-entity graph, judges entity disambiguation (sameAs/Wikidata), and assesses topical authority via the internal link graph.
- [structured-data](structured-data.md) -- Judges whether an AI can extract key facts from the page's schema.org markup, llms.txt, and meta tags, and identifies missing or mismatched schema.
